Alan Walker

Alan Olav Walker is a Norwegian DJ and record producer. Raised in Bergen, Norway, he began making music using feedback from his fans online around 2012, subsequently posting several videos on YouTube and SoundCloud.

Born: 24 August 1997 (age 27 years), Northampton, United Kingdom

Also known as: DJ Walkzz
